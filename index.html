My Story

1999 - USCG

I enlisted in the United States Coast Guard during my last year of High School to take advantage of the GI Bill. It was a challenging adventure that also helped pay for college.


1

2003 - College

Everyone said I needed to go so I went. The GI Bill helped pay for most of it. I started at Des Moines Area Community College and finished my journey with a Bachelor of Science in Finance from Iowa State University.

2

2007 - Financial Crisis

I graduated in December of 2007. The irony of my chosen major would soon be front and center. This was a tuff time for myself and a lot of other people. I survived and ended up going back to Des Moines Area Community College to learn more about the world of Information Technology.


3

2011 - Corporate America

I finally lucked out and got my first IT job, but eventually the parent company decided to outsource everything related to IT. Along the way I survived a hectic merger followed by the spin-off of a new company. In the beginning I felt valued and learned a lot, but twoard the end I was always worried about losing my job and I felt like I needed to make a change.

4

2018 - Making changes and taking chances

I thought my job was in jepordy and I was tired of living in fear. I did the best I could to find a suitable replacement but it ended up being a step in the wrong direction. I ended up resigning, selling almost everything I owned, and retreating back to Iowa.

5

2019 - The Pandemic

What an interesting time for all of us. I spent my first year at home sheltering in place and learning everything I could about Amazon Web Services. I got some AWS exposure before I left Corteva Agriscience. Cloud native application developement was a hot topic and I was genuinely interested.

6

Present

I want to be a JavaScript Developer and I want to build on AWS. I try to learn something new and make some measurable progress toward those goals every week.
